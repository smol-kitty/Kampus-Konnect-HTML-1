ABOUT CAREER COUNSELLING AND PLACEMENT CELL (CCPC)

The Career Counselling and Placement Cell (CCPC) of the Faculty of Commerce, The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda, has been actively bridging the gap between students and potential employers since 2014. It fosters a strong Industry-Institute Interface, providing students with career insights, industry requirements, and skill enhancement through training programs, mock interviews, and industry-specific workshops. CCPC also facilitates MOUs with industry partners under CSR initiatives to support employability enhancement. With increasing student registrations and industry collaborations, CCPC has successfully placed over 1100 students in reputed companies like TCS, ICICI Bank, HDFC Sales, Tech Mahindra, and Reliance Nippon. Through continuous efforts, CCPC ensures that students gain practical exposure and industry-aligned skills, paving the way for promising career opportunities.
